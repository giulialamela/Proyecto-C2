The increase in the generation of garbage in the environment promoted that, in 2017, Underground Clothing was born, by the hand of Maria Gonzalez, where they give a second chance to different discarded materials of clothing, to make unique pieces, promoting the circular economy and responsible consumption in favor of caring for the environment. They are garments made by hand with recycled or recovered textile materials. Definitely we want to make an impact.
